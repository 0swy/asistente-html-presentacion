# Design Doc: Persistent History Panel and Sliding Exit Animations

## Context
The user wants to maintain focus during their presentation by removing completed sentences from the main view while keeping them accessible in a history panel.

## Requirements
- **Sliding Exit**: Completed items slide right and disappear.
- **Dynamic History**: Floating button with counter.
- **Classified Panel**: View completed items grouped by category.
- **Styling**: Glassmorphism side panel.
